Marinated Skirt Steak

Top Tier Marinated Skirt Steak

A great flank steak marinade like this one is important if you want a tender, juicy, flavorful steak. Make sure you marinate your flank steak for at least 2 hours for best results or longer if you have time. This wonderful quick and easy recipe also works great when the steak is sliced and used for fajitas.

Marinade

  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ⅓ cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • cloves garlic, minced

Steps:

  1. Gather All ingredients
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Whisk together oil, soy sauce, vinegar, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, garlic, and pepper for marinade until thoroughly combined.
  4. Pour marinade over flank steak
  5. When ready to cook, preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat
  6. Cook steak on the preheated grill for about 5 minutes per side
